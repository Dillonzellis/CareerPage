Why Choose LSCU & Affiliates

At LSCU & Affiliates, we are driven by service and our commitment to credit unions. We work tirelessly to strengthen, support, and advocate for the credit union industry. Come work with a team who supports a philosophy of ‘people helping people’.

Competitive Benefits

We believe in supporting our team and their families by offering competitive and comprehensive benefits plans. We offer a variety of choices including, health, dental and vision insurance, parental leave, paid vacation, and paid holidays, life insurance, and flex-spending accounts. We also offer a rich retirement benefit with a competitive match.

Professional Development

Gain the confidence to advance your career with our professional development, tuition reimbursements, and our Emerging Leaders Program (ELP). Our ELP is an intensive, and comprehensive leadership journey that will accelerate your growth and increase your leadership capacities through targeted training led by industry professionals.

Work-Life Balance

Take control of your work-life synergy with flexible schedules and a supportive work environment. Get more satisfaction from work with gym reimbursement, discounts on tickets to theme parks, performances, sporting events, and much more. Enjoy better health and wellness with our integrated wellness portal with free 24/7 access to certified physicians with a telemedicine platform.

Build Equity

We are determined to create an environment where team members can thrive by setting a high bar for diversity and inclusion and fostering a fair, collaborative, high-performing culture. We believe that a diverse and inclusive environment is a breeding ground for accomplishment. Leveraging our strengths, knowing our weaknesses, and being honest about both is the best way to succeed.

Team Engagement

Our engagement committee provides the opportunity for teams across our footprint to get to know one another and nurture personal relationships while boosting workplace morale. From coffee break networking, sports outings, and social events, our committees work together to create engaging experiences for everyone.

Our Values

Our culture is driven by our values. Our team members, whether out in the field building relationships or in the office producing data-rich reports, are leading innovation, collaborating, and influencing decisions and decision-makers alike.
